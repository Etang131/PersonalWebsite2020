General Insructions

  • Start whenever you need to in the progression for each section. For example, some people might start with 1 for pull, push, and hinge, and 2 for squat.
  • Do reps until failure or you hit the target. The target is listed with the movement (eg pull-ups x10) Push and Hinge targets are all 10 reps.
  • Between reps, take a few deep breaths and start the next movement immediately if able. If you need more time between reps, then take it. Remember, quality over quantity.
  • Rest for at least two minutes between circuits when first starting out. As you progress, reduce the rest to 1-2 minutes.
  • Level up when you are able to hit the target number of reps for a movement for all four circuits

Warm up

To be safe you should always do some streaching, and then pick one of the following:

  1. Jumpting Jacks
  2. Burpees
  3. Mountain Climbers
  4. Jump rope

Pull

Do ten reps of whichever level you are on of:

  1. Bent-leg bodyweight rows with dip bar
  2. Straight-leg bodyweight rows with dip bar
  3. Pull up negatives x5
  4. Assisted pull-ups (with resistance band)
  5. Pull-ups
  6. Tucked front-level pull-ups

Squat

  1. Half squat (thighs almost parallel) x30
  2. Full squat x30 (optional calf raises)
  3. Hop squat x20
  4. Lunge x20
  5. Switching lunges
  6. Tuck-jump squats

Push

Do ten reps of whichever level you are on of:

  1. Wall push-ups
  2. Incline push-ups (use stairs, chair, etc
  3. Standard push-ups
  4. Diamond push-ups
  5. Archer push-ups
  6. Clapping push-ups

Hinge

Do ten reps of whichever level you are on of:

  1. Lying Tucks (lying on the floor with legs extended, tuck your knees into your chest)
  2. Lying leg raises
  3. Hanging knee raises
  4. Hanging leg raises
  5. Inverted leg raises (headstand with leg 'raises')

Cool down

Do some light movement, but don't just sit on the couch as that can be bad for your health.
